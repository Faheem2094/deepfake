About FaceFortify

Welcome to FaceFortify, where we are at the forefront of combating the dangers of deepfake technology. Our mission is to leverage advanced AI and machine learning techniques to detect and prevent face-swapped deepfake videos, ensuring digital authenticity and protecting against digital deception.
